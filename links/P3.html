The Seasoned Spoon is a celebration of flavorful, seasonal cooking that brings simple yet delicious recipes to life.

Whether you're preparing a cozy dinner or impressing guests, The Seasoned Spoon inspires creativity in every meal.

Pork Sisig

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish made from chopped pig’s face, ears, and liver,
seasoned with calamansi, chili, and mayonnaise, then served sizzling on a hot plate.
